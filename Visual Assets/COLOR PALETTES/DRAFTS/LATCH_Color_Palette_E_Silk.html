Application Guide
App UI
Ivory canvas, Linen for card backgrounds. Charcoal text. Interactive blue for CTAs. The warm-cool interplay prevents the interface from feeling sterile or cold.
Marketing / Social
Linen backgrounds with Pewter accent lines. Charcoal headlines. This palette photographs well — warm tones look natural in lifestyle and food photography contexts.
Investor / Print
Charcoal ground with Pewter accents for financial slides. Ivory for data panels. Prints on warm stock without any color shift — already warm-calibrated.

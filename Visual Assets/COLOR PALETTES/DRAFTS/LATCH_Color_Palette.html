Application Guide
App UI
Snow canvas, silver for cards/dividers, Ink for text, Accent for interactive elements and CTAs.
Marketing / Social
Blue 50 or gradient backgrounds. Ice Blue for accents and dividers. Ink for headlines. Keep it minimal.
Investor / Print
Dark backgrounds for authority. Ice Blue for accent lines and key metrics. Snow for data cards and contrast.

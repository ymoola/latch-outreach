Consumers want to go out. Merchants need them to. But the economics are broken on both sides. Consumers feel every bill. Merchants spend thousands on marketing that doesn't track to a single table. The tools that exist — UberEats, Groupon, Google Ads — either extract value from merchants or send deal-seekers with no loyalty. Nobody is building for the relationship between a great local business and a customer who keeps coming back.

The Solution

Latch connects
consumers with
local businesses.
Simply.

Subscribers pay a monthly or annual fee to unlock curated BOGO-style offers across dining, wellness, fitness, and more. Merchants get guaranteed exposure to paying subscribers, trackable foot traffic, and zero delivery commissions. No one loses. Everyone wins.

The coupon cap — 3 per merchant per year — protects merchants from deal abuse while giving members genuine, recurring value. It's structured so that the best way to get more is to explore more. That's the engine.

The Loyalty Engine

Rewarding
discovery.
Protecting
merchants.

Most discount platforms die because merchants get destroyed. Groupon merchants famously lost money — one viral post from a small bakery nearly tanked the company. Latch is structurally different.

The 3-coupon cap means merchants never absorb more than three redemptions per subscriber per year. The loyalty system means subscribers are rewarded for exploring — not for exploiting their favourite spot. The annual reset means every year is a fresh start.

This is the Groupon problem, solved by design.

Revenue Model

Three compounding
revenue streams.

Each stream reinforces the others. More merchants attract more subscribers. More subscribers justify higher merchant deposits. Payment processing monetises every transaction.

01
Live at Launch

Merchant Onboarding

Annual non-refundable deposit per merchant. Founding Partners get Year 1 fully waived as a launch incentive, transitioning to standard pricing once spots fill. Premium placement available for additional fee.

$2,000–$2,500/yr per merchant
02
Live at Launch

Consumer Subscriptions

Monthly and annual plans across four tiers: General, Student/Senior, Premium, and Traveler. Annual subscribers save 25–30% vs monthly. High ARPU Traveler tier adds meaningful incremental revenue from tourist traffic.

$5.99 to $49.99/mo · Four tiers
03
Phase 2

Payment Processing

In-app payments with auto-calculated discounts at checkout. A small processing fee from merchants on each transaction creates the third compounding revenue layer — monetising every redemption event on the platform.

Small % per transaction · Phase 2
